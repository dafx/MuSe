Frequently Asked Questions
Why should I provide my Last.fm Username?
Providing your Last.fm Username will allow the system to get an even broader picture of your taste of music, given you are using the Last.fm service for some time. We will only access data that is publicly available on your Last.fm account. With the additional information you will be able to use two recommenders that are only available to users providing their Last.fm name.
Why can't I play any of the recommended songs?
The most probable reason is that you haven't installed the Spotify music streaming client on your computer. As we are using this service to allow direct listening of the recommended songs you will not be able to do so without Spotify installed. To overcome this issue either install spotify or look for the recommended songs in your favorite music service.
Are recommendations only improving over night?
To improve recommendations we have to take your newly made ratings into account. As this task takes some time and computation power we are performing it every night. This will keep your MuSe experience at its best and we hope you will come back to look for new recommendations the next day!
How can I change my password?
To change your password open the user menu (user icon in the upper right corner) and click on "My Profile". Then click on the "Change Profile" button and easily change you password.
How can I delete my account?
To delete your account open the user menu (user icon in the upper right corner) and click on "My Profile". Then click "Delete Account" and type in your current password.
