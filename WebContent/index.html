To get to know your taste we need some information about you. When creating an account consider to provide your Last.fm name to get even better recommendations.

Listen & Rate

Get lists of recommended songs and listen to them right away. To get the best MuSe experience we suggest to use Spotify in order to listen to the songs.

By rating songs you will get better recommendations the next day, as we recompute your taste each night.

Get Recommendations

After rating enough songs you will start to discover great songs and artists. Since we get to know your taste better and better you will get recommendations that are directly tailored to you.
